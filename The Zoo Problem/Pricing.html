Pricing and Passes

Membership Pricing

Standard Membership: $5 annual donation.

Bronze Membership: $10 annual donation.

Silver Membership: $25 annual donation.

Gold Membership: $40 annual donation.

Rainbow Membership: $65 annual donation.

*Donations are only charged on an annual basis to the debit/credit card on file. All Membership Donation funds are used to provide care for the resident animals.*

Passes

    Lion Show Pass

  • An all access pass for your entire visiting party to watch the resident Lions wake up and get morning enrichment time. Activities you'll witness include, but are not limited to: morning feeding, fetch, vocal lessons, training exercises, etc.
  • $10 per Pass.

    • Parent Night Pass

  • An all-inclusive pass for the weekly Parent Night! Parent Night is a 2-hour open event for all visiting parents holding a Zoo Membership. This pass includes free ticket to the open bar, free ticket to the Buffet Bar, free DJ song request and exclusive after hours R-Rated Zoo Tour.
  • $40 per Pass. Each Pass is valid for one person.
    *See the Events Page for more details*

    • Gator Meet & Greet Pass

  • An all access pass for your entire visitng party to get up close and personal with Mika the Alligator! This one-of-a-kind show allows your visitng party to personal interact, play and pet Mika! She loves to nuzzle humans and always enjoys stimulation from visitors.
  • $15 per Pass. Event is fully supervised by Zoo Pros.

    • Penguin Meet & Greet Pass

  • An all access pass for your entire visiting party to meet the resident penguins and get a chance to pet them! You'll even have a chance to be gifted a pebble from one of the Penguins if you make a special impression on them!
  • $15 per Pass. Event is fully supervised by Zoo Pros.

    • Adults-Only Predator Hunting Show

  • Here at Gingertopia Zoo we don't believe in supressing the natural instincts of our resident animals, this is why we host a one-hour, Adults-Only, Hunting Show for each of our Predator Animals. The show allows visitors to watch our predators stalk, hunt down, and kill their prey as they naturally do in the wild.
  • $25 per Pass. Event is held in a secured viewing room.
    *See the Events Page for a list of viewable hunting shows*
